Welcome to the Hidden Cup 5 Player guessing competition!

Here you can guess which hero was played by which participant. All guesses will be published on a scoreboard when the competition is over. The persons with most correct guesses win!

There is no prize. But you may get the right to brag about being at the top of the scoreboard.

If you have special knowledge, that means if you are an admin for HC5 or a player in the top 16, you can still participate, but correct guesses will be a lot less impressive.

How do you want to assign players to heroes?

Please assign each Player from the bottom to a Hero on the top by dragging the Player onto the spot below the Hero.

Please assign a Player to each Hero by selecting a name from the selection menu below each hero.

Once you are done, click the Confirm and Next button at the bottom.

How would you like to appear on the leaderboard?

For example the username you use on Twitch or Discord
